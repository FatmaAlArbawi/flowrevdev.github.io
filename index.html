The Problem

Every year, 15 million people suffer strokes globally, with 87% being ischemic and preventable. Existing solutions intervene too late, costing $52,000/year out-of-pocket and causing lifelong disabilities. Every 4 minutes, someone in the U.S. dies from a stroke. 25% of stroke survivors will suffer another stroke within 5 years.

Our Solution

Flowrev is a wearable medical device that detects and prevents recurrent ischemic strokes before the clot makes its way into smaller branches in the brain. It uses miniaturized ultrasound and spectroscopy to monitor:

  • Blood Flow Velocity
  • Oxygen Saturation (SpO2)
  • Bilirubin Levels
  • Blood Serum Density

Key Features:

  • Wearable Ultrasound Technology
  • Non-Invasive Detection
  • Rapid Clot Dissolution (Vortex Ultrasound)
  • Use outside of clinical settings

Detecting & Preventing Blood Clots in Carotid Artery

Flowrev detects blood clots forming in the carotid arteries before they reach the brain. Our spectrometery readings are compared to thresholds to analyze stroke risk.

Our prevention method uses vortex ultrasound for rapid clot dissolution, which was shown to dissolve clots in vitro in under 30 minutes.

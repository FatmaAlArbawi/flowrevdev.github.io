Flowrev

Preventing Strokes Before They Strike

The Problem

Every year, 15 million people suffer strokes globally, with 87% being ischemic and preventable. Current solutions intervene too late, costing $52,000/year out-of-pocket and causing lifelong disabilities. Every 4 minutes, someone in the U.S. dies from a stroke.

Our Solution

Flowrev is a wearable medical device that detects and prevents recurrent ischemic strokes. It uses miniaturized ultrasound and spectroscopy to monitor:

  • Blood Flow Velocity
  • Oxygen Saturation (SpO2)
  • Bilirubin Levels
  • Blood Serum Density

Flowrev detects blood clots forming in the carotid arteries before they reach the brain.

  • Wearable Ultrasound Technology
  • Non-Invasive Detection
  • Rapid Clot Dissolution (Vortex Ultrasound)
  • Use outside of clinical settings

Market Opportunity

We are addressing a $3.53B global market for recurrent ischemic stroke prevention, saving hospitals $10-15 billion annually on recurrent stroke treatments.
